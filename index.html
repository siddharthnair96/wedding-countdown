Our Story

We were in the same college, same academic year — yet somehow never crossed paths. That changed in 2018 during our management training at Taj Connemara in Chennai. She ran the kitchen. I ran the restaurant. Between service hours, stolen breaks, and quiet conversations in the back corridor… something unexpected began. What started as friendship slowly became something much bigger. And now, years later… we’re finally making it official.
