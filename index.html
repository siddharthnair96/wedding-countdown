Our Story

We spent four years in the same college, in the same batch — and somehow never met. By the fourth year she had at least heard about me though, mostly as “the guy who went to Dubai for his internship.” Our actual introduction didn’t happen until 2018 during our management training at Taj Connemara in Chennai. She ran the kitchen. I ran the restaurant. Which meant we technically worked together… and argued across the pass often enough that the kitchen and service teams were probably taking bets on how that would end. Somewhere between service hours, stolen breaks, and quiet conversations in the back corridor, we started talking a little more. About five months into the training program, friendship quietly turned into something else. Not long after that, life started moving in different directions. In 2020 I stepped away from the hotel industry and moved into the world of e‑commerce. What followed was several years of airports, time zones, and long‑distance calls — including a cross‑continent stretch when Switzerland entered the story. In July 2024, after several months of her politely reminding me that she still didn’t have a ring, I finally decided to fix that. One evening we went out for dinner while, behind the scenes, my dad and a friend were busy decorating the house. On the drive back we even had a very serious conversation about rings — during which I carefully lowered expectations. When she walked through the door, the house was filled with candles and rose petals. And yes… I got down on one knee. And after years of airports, time zones, career pivots, and a healthy number of kitchen‑versus‑restaurant arguments — we’ve decided this arrangement should probably be permanent.
